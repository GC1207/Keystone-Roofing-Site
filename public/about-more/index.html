Our Process

At Keystone Roofing, we specialize in restoring commercial roofs using high-quality Conklin products. Our process is designed to extend the life of your existing roof, improve energy efficiency, and save you money on costly replacements.

  • We start by thoroughly inspecting your roof and cleaning it with Conklin's specialized cleaners to ensure optimal adhesion of the coating system.
  • Next we apply a primer to improve the adhesion of the coating and protect against rust (for metal roofs).
  • We then reinforce all seams, fasteners, and penetrations with Conklin's high-strength Spunflex fabric and Kwik Kaulk to prevent leaks.
  • Finally, we apply Conklin's high-quality, reflective top coat to your entire roof surface.

You can choose from a variety of coating options, including PUMA XL, Benchmark, Rapid Roof III, Rapid Roof HV, or Equinox, depending on your specific needs and budget.

Why Conklin

Conklin's roof restoration systems are cost-effective, saving you up to 50% compared to the cost of a new roof. Plus, their cool roof systems can significantly reduce your energy costs, providing long-term savings.

Conklin's cool roof systems are highly reflective, reducing roof temperatures by up to 40% and decreasing the amount of heat transferred into your building. This leads to a cooler, more comfortable work environment and less strain on your HVAC system.

Conklin's ENERGY STAR-rated cool roof systems can significantly reduce your building's cooling costs. By reflecting up to 85% of the sun's heat, these systems minimize the energy needed to cool your building, resulting in lower utility bills and reduced environmental impact.
